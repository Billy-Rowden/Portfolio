About Me

Hello! My name is Billy, I'm 24 and I'm an aspiring Front-end web developer from the UK. Due to my parents work, I grew up in Hong Kong and Dubai, and now I'm based in Devon in the southwest of the UK. I love picking up new skills that have elements of problem solving, that challenge and stimulate me mentally. In my down time I like to keep fit by doing physical activities, particularly ones that, while involving full body fitness and coordination, also stimulates the brain, such as climbing, and my personal favourite, Trampwall which stems from trampolining. TrampWall is a hybrid sport that mixes traditional trampolining skills with elements of parkour and freerunning.
