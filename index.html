About Me

Hello! My name is Billy, I'm 24 and I'm an aspiring Front-end web developer from the UK. Due to my parents work, I grew up in Hong Kong and Dubai, and now I'm based in Devon in the southwest of the UK. I love picking up new skills that have elements of problem solving, that challenge and stimulate me mentally. The thing I most enjoy in web development so far is debugging problems within code to figure out what the problem is and how to best fix it in a way that will achieve the desired outcome for the website. I hope as a result of completing the Skills for Life bootcamp for Front-End Web Development, that I will be able to find work in this industry.

In my down time I like to keep fit by doing physical activities, particularly ones that, while involving full body fitness and coordination, also stimulates the brain, such as climbing, and my personal favourite, Trampwall which stems from trampolining. TrampWall is a hybrid sport that mixes traditional trampolining skills with elements of parkour and freerunning. I also love spending time with friends doing sports or skills that I haven't tried before and that they excel in, so that I can progress quickly and learn from someone that is already knowledgeable in that area.
